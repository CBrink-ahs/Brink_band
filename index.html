5 Seconds of Summer

Who Is 5 Seconds of Summer

5 Seconds of summer is an Australian pop rock band from Sydney, New South Wales formed in 2011. The Band was orginally YouTube celebreties posting videos of themselves singing covers in 2011-2012. until later found by Louis Tomlinson (a band member of One Direction) in 2012 when he tweeted one of their covers on twitter. 5 Seconds of Summer later went on tour with One Direction on the Take Me Home tour in early 2013 where their carrier started to take off. They have since released three studio albums, seven EPs and one live album headlining in three tours.
